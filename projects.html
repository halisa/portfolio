PROJECTS

cloudy days

A cat game about mental health and being a friend. The purpose is to bring youth awareness of mental illness in a kid-friendly style. Especially with mass media, the perception of mental illness is often warped in ways that don’t reflect reality. By attempting to dispel misconceptions of mental health that may begin when children are young, it can encourage more understanding and discussion of what mental illnesses really are.

Developed in Python using the Pygame library. Used Agile, Scrum, and Kanban project management methodologies. Code can be found on Github.



northbound

A college preparation website geared to low-income and first-generation high school students. The site provides information on how to prepare for college later on and how the college application process works. The scholarships tab is a page filled with resources on scholarship/internship opportunities and how to find them.

Developed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Launched with Github. Code can be found on Github.
