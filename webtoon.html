WEBTOON PROJECT

I am writing and illustrating a webcomic that will be published on Webtoons (but not yet!). I have been developing the characters since last summer. At first, I wrote a dark mystery and thriller story, but I scrapped most of it for a lighter-toned romance and drama story because that it what has interested me lately. However, I still kept the defining characteristic of the main character, so I didn't entirely scrap everything. The theme I will be exploring is identity and how culture can play a role.

You can find out more about this project at this Instagram page: My Webtoon Project


Blurb

A Chinese international student comes over to America for a better education and a chance to free herself from her demanding family. After a visit to the corporate afterlife, Yulie is stuck not only between two worlds, but also with an impossible contract that can threaten her entire existence.
