about me

I am a rising senior and International Baccalaureate diploma candidate at Kent-Meridian High School with a strong interest in computer science and art/design. During the past summer, I was a student in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at F5 Networks. I learned how to code and gained more knowledge of how the technology industry works. After I graduate high school, I intend on earning a bachelor's of science degree in Computer Science. In my free time, I enjoy doodling, making games, creating art and comics and reading young adult novels.
