my story


As an american-born daughter of vietnamese immigrants, I grew up in shifting households and navigating the liminal spaces of being asian american and queer. The ways that I experience the world lead me to create in order to connect to myself, process, grieve, reflect, and reimagine.

My creative passions are collaging, doodling, digital/traditional painting, graphic design, illustration, and sticker-making. I also love to knit, crochet, read books, and draw comics!

Currently, I am working as a design intern and community advocate on campus. I am majoring in computer science and transformative art and design with a creative writing minor at Pacific Lutheran University. I am looking for neat projects all year round and design or swe internships for summer 2023. Feel free to contact me for business inquiries below.
