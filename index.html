my story


As an american-born daughter of vietnamese immigrants, I grew up in shifting households and navigating the liminal spaces of being asian american and queer. The ways that I experience the world lead me to create in order to connect to myself, process, grieve, reflect, and reimagine.

My creative passions are design, doodling, illustration, and digital/traditional painting. I also love to knit, crochet, read books, and draw comics!

Currently, I am working as a design intern and community advocate while majoring in computer science and gender, race and sexuality studies with studio arts and writing minors. I am looking for neat projects all year round and design or swe internships for summer 2022. Feel free to contact me for business inquiries below.
