About Me

I am a rising senior and International Baccalaureate diploma candidate at Kent-Meridian High School with a strong interest in computer science and art/design. As a current student in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at F5 Networks, I am learning how to code and gaining more knowledge of how the technology industry works.
